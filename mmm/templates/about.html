About

General

The Magic Manuscript Maker is a tool utilized by the Philosophy and the Mind Sciences journal. It builds an integral part of the typesetting and article production phase. The primary development of this tool was led by Thomas Jurczyk for the website and web application, and Felix Kopecky for LaTeX and PDF rendering with Pandoc.

If you have any suggestions or questions, please send us an e-mail.

Technical Details

The Magic Manuscript Maker is based on a mix of Pandoc, Flask (Python), and Docker. The software is open source and hosted here: GitHub repository.
